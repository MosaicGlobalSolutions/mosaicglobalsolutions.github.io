    About Mosaic

    Mosaic Global Solutions was founded to help extend collaboration to clients and partners to ensure successful business results between international counterparts, leaders and multinational teams.

    A holistic approach brings clients beyond culture, into the intricacies of how international business works. This allows Mosaic to help solve complex people, process, and systemic global challenges.

    Services

    Key Note Addresses

    Dynamic and interactive, the 60 - 120 minute keynote address is for groups of all sizes will provide to participants the Culture Map framework for thinking about cultural differences as well as offering practical strategies for working globally. Participants learn to decode how people think, lead, and get things done when working in an international environment, and develop strategies to manage these differences effectively.

    Multicultural Team-Building

    Intact teams representing various cultures will explore cultural similarities and differences that impact day-to-day success; map their cultural similarities and differences utilizing the Culture Map framework and develop strategies for increasing team synergy the ability to reach their business goals.

    Global Leadership

    Utilizing coach-certified techniques and expensive research in global leadership, this customized coaching is especially suited for leaders who have recently taken an increasingly global role.

    Cross-Cultural

    How do cultural differences impact how work gets done? This session, available in full, half-day and virtual formats, will leverage the Culture Map framework to help participants identify how they are similar and different from their international counterparts, the impact of those differences and strategies that can help them to be just as successful working globally as they would be in their home country.
